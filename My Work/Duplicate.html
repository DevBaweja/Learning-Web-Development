Information about birds

  1. Birds have feathers, wings, lay eggs and are warm blooded.
  2. There are around 10000 different species of birds worldwide.
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Aves


Information about ducks

  1. Ducks are birds that are also called “waterfowls” because they are normally found in places with water like marshes, oceans, rivers, ponds, and lakes.
  2. Ducks are related to geese and swans, but the duck is the smallest of them all.
  3. Ducks have shorter necks and wings than other waterfowl, and they also have a stout body.
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Aves

Information about dogs

  1. Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated mammals, not natural wild animals.
  2. They were originally bred from wolves
  3. They have been bred by humans for a long time, and were the first animals ever to be domesticated.
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: . Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
